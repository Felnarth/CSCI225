Introduction

Welcome to my CSCI225 github page.
My name is Aaron Derr. I am a senior in the applied computer science (no concentration) curriculum. I attended Clemson University for 3 years in the
Computer Engineering curriculum. Computer Engineering couldn't be any more different from computer science, in terms of enjoyment and focus, than Computer Science
in my opinion. I became tired of electronics, power, circuitry, and hardware, and decided that I enjoyed programming so much more. I returned to Aiken and switched
to Computer Science.
I work at Savannah River Site in Savannah River Nuclear Solutions.
My job primarily involves database design and management (SQL & FileMaker), desktop application development (c# and filemaker),
and web development (react js, node, asp.net core). My hobbies are reading, programming, and playing video games.
